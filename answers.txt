Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: It helps us track code oer time and allows us to save different versions so we can go back to the previous ones if all goes wrong. Also can be useful when working with other people on a project.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data Science could be applied to almost every field. For example in health care you can use data science to discover possible symptoms of a disease or test effectiveness of a drug.
